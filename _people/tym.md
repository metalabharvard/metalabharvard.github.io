Tym Yee is a visual artist and PhD candidate at Macquarie University in Sydney, Australia. His doctoral research investigates the emergence of internal innovation cultures in corporate organisations, questioning what becomes of the future of work when constant technological disruption is expected of every employee across the knowledge economy. Methodologically, he draws on new media theory, ethnography, and creative practice to bring ubiquitous white-collar work settings into contact with speculative art making.
